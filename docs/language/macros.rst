Macros
======

Macros are used to simplify the entry of multiple tag commands.

Built-in Macros
---------------

Chemistry
~~~~~~~~~

+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| Macro            | Formatted Text                       |
+==================+======================================+
| ``@1H``          | :sup:`1`\ H                          |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| ``@13C``         | :sup:`13`\ C                         |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| ``@15N``         | :sup:`15`\ N                         |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| ``@19F``         | :sup:`19`\ F                         |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| ``@31P``         | :sup:`31`\ P                         |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
| ``@H2O``         | H\ :sub:`2`\ O                       |
+------------------+--------------------------------------+
